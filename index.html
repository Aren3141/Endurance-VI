What is F1 in Schools?

F1 in Schools is the world's leading global educational program that raises awareness of Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) among students. Teams of students aged 9 to 19 deploy CAD/CAM software to collaborate, design, analyze, manufacture, test, and then race miniature compressed air-powered cars made from a model block.

It's not just about speed; teams are also judged on their car's design, a verbal presentation, their portfolio, and their marketing and sponsorship efforts. It's a holistic challenge that mirrors the processes of a real-world Formula 1 team.
